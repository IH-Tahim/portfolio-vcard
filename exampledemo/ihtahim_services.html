How I Work

A 7-step system
built for results.

Most freelancers jump straight to execution. I start with diagnosis. Every step below is designed to eliminate guesswork and maximise your return — before we spend a single taka on ads.

01
The Discovery Call

We start with a quick chat to diagnose your business. I want to understand your core pain points, your ideal customer, and the psychological triggers that actually make them buy.

02
Market & Competitor Teardown

Before we build anything, I reverse-engineer your top competitors. I look at their ad copy, their landing pages, and their offers to see exactly what they're doing right — and more importantly, where they're leaving money on the table for us to grab.

03
The Technical Audit

I look under your hood. I audit your site's code for speed and mobile responsiveness, check your SEO health, and map out exactly where your current sales funnel is leaking traffic.

04
The Blueprint

I come back to you with a visual map of the exact journey your customer will take — from the first ad impression to the final checkout — along with a clear timeline and proposal.

05
Build & Execute

This is where my dev skills kick in. I build out blazing-fast, custom landing pages, set up advanced server-side tracking that standard marketers can't touch, and write ad copy using psychological principles designed to convert.

06
Launch & Iterate

We go live — but the work is just starting. I aggressively track the data, use heatmaps, and A/B test the layouts and messaging to constantly push your conversion rates higher.

07
Scale

Once we have the data proving the funnel is profitable, we open the taps. We scale the ad budget efficiently, expanding into new audiences and maximising your return on investment.
