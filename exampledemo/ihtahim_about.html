My Journey
Chapter 01
The Science Student Who Fell in Love With the Web

I studied BSc Chemistry at Mohammadpur Kendriya College under National University — a science background with zero connection to tech. But I was always drawn to design. One day I stumbled on web design and something clicked. I started teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch — YouTube, Google, Udemy, Quora — whatever resource I could find.

Chapter 02
From Designer to Full-Stack Developer

Once I understood how websites look, I wanted to understand how they work. That curiosity pushed me to learn WordPress and Elementor, then deeper into MERN Stack development through Programming Hero. I built real projects — CPL Express's document management system, multiple WordPress sites like Aftab Overseas and Nojor TV. Code became my tool for solving real problems.

Chapter 03
The Marketing Epiphany at Spotless4u

Joining Spotless4u Cleaning Services in Canberra as IT Manager changed everything. Managing their website wasn't enough — I needed to bring in clients. I ran Google Ads, implemented SEO, built an online quote system, and tracked cleaner schedules through apps. I realised: developers don't speak marketing, and marketers don't speak development. I speak both — and that's my edge.

Chapter 04 — Now
Exploring the AI Frontier

Today I'm actively exploring AI-powered marketing — AI website builders, AI video tools, chatbot integration, and marketing automation. I believe the next great marketers won't just use AI — they'll know how to connect it to their full stack. That's exactly where I'm heading.
